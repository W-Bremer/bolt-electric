Frequently asked questions

What is the battery capacity?

Our current MagCharge line features a 5000mAh capacity, providing ample power to keep your devices charged throughout the day.

Is it MagSafe compatible?

Yes! All our MagCharge products are fully MagSafe compatible, featuring strong N52-grade magnets for a secure connection and efficient wireless charging.

What charging speeds does it support?

Our chargers support 15W wireless charging and 20W wired Power Delivery (PD) via the USB-C port for fast and versatile charging options.

What's included in the box?

Each Bolt Electric MagCharge comes with the charger unit, a USB-C cable, a soft drawstring pouch, a quick start guide, all in eco-friendly packaging.
