Charge Cooler, Play Longer

The Bolt GameCool Edition is specifically engineered for mobile gamers and power users. Featuring advanced NTC chip technology, it actively regulates temperature to charge up to 6x cooler during intense gaming or streaming sessions. Maintain peak performance without overheating.

  • Advanced Cooling: NTC chip ensures significantly cooler charging under load.
  • Ergonomic Design: Comfortable grip and matte finish for extended use.
  • Reliable Power: 5000mAh capacity with 15W wireless and 20W USB-C PD charging.
