Frequently asked questions

What is the warranty on Bolt Electric products?

All Bolt Electric products come with a standard 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Please refer to our warranty page for full details.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Expedited options are available at checkout. International shipping times vary.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused products in their original packaging. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Are your products safe?

Absolutely. Our products undergo rigorous 3M-level safety checks and include features like NTC overheat protection and auto shut-off for overcharge protection.
